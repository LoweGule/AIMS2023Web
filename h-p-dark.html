Tsukuba Daigaku, or Tsukuba University is a university in Tsukuba

City, Japan. This University is one of the Universities that work

together with SEAMEO RIHED on the AIMS Program, which means

that they work together with South East Asian Countries, including

Malaysia, Brunei Darrusalam, Indonesia, Philippines, Thailand,

Japan, South Korea, Singapore, and Vietnam.
